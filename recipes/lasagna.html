Lasagna

>

Description

Ingredients

  • Meat: This super meaty lasagna has sweet Italian sausage and lean ground beef.
  • Onion and garlic: An onion and two cloves of garlic are cooked with the meat to add tons of flavor.
  • Tomato products: You'll need a can of crushed tomatoes, two cans of tomato sauce, and two cans of tomato paste.
  • Sugar: Two tablespoons of white sugar add subtle sweetness and enhance the flavor of the sauce.
  • Spices and seasonings: This lasagna recipe is flavored with fresh parsley, dried basil leaves, salt, Italian seasoning, fennel seeds, and black pepper.
  • Lasagna noodles: Use store-bought or homemade lasagna noodles.
  • Cheeses: Parmesan, mozzarella, and ricotta cheese make this lasagna extra decadent.
  • Egg: An egg helps bind the ricotta so it doesn't ooze out of the lasagna when you cut into it.

Steps

  1. Cook the sweet Italian sausage and lean ground beef with chopped onion and minced garlic until browned.
  2. Add crushed tomatoes, tomato sauce, and tomato paste to the meat mixture.
  3. Stir in two tablespoons of white sugar for subtle sweetness.
  4. Season the sauce with fresh parsley, dried basil leaves, salt, Italian seasoning, fennel seeds, and black pepper.
  5. Simmer the sauce for about 30 minutes to allow the flavors to meld.
  6. While the sauce simmers, boil the lasagna noodles until al dente, then drain and set aside.
  7. In a separate bowl, combine Parmesan, mozzarella, ricotta cheese, and one beaten egg.
  8. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).
  9. Spread a layer of meat sauce in the bottom of a baking dish.
  10. Layer noodles over the sauce, followed by a layer of the cheese mixture.
  11. Repeat the layers until all ingredients are used, ending with a layer of meat sauce on top.
  12. Cover the baking dish with foil and bake for 25 minutes.
  13. Remove the foil and bake for an additional 25 minutes, or until the lasagna is bubbly and golden.
  14. Let the lasagna cool for a few minutes before slicing and serving.
